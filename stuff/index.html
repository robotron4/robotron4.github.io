what's the tao you might ask? The Tao Te Ching, written more than two thousand years ago, is one of the true classics of spiritual literature. It is a guide to cultivating a life of peace, serenity, and compassion. Through aphorisms and parable, it leads readers toward the Tao, or the “Way”: harmony with the life force of the universe.(Text sourced from https://www.organism.earth/library/document/tao-te-ching)
